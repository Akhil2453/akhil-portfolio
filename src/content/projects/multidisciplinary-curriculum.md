## Breadth of subjects

The curriculum portfolio spans commerce, management, databases, programming, data science, cloud, electronics, IoT, embedded systems, robotics, blockchain, mobile applications and research methods.

## Design principles

Content is developed around progressive learning, clear outcomes, authentic examples, executable activities and assessment alignment. For online delivery, the work also considers narration, visual sequencing, learner workload and independent study support.
