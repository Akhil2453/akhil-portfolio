## Project direction

FloraBot was framed around a simple proposition: one mobile platform can support more than one environmental mission. The design combines observation, sensor-based reporting and controlled physical interaction.

The project is also used as a teaching vehicle for system architecture, embedded networking, interface design and responsible technology development.
