## Approach

Competition mentoring was organised as an engineering development process. Teams moved from problem interpretation to solution architecture, mechanism selection, prototype testing, integration and communication.

Rather than replacing learner decisions, mentoring focused on asking better technical questions: What evidence supports this mechanism? What happens when the load changes? Can the subsystem be repaired quickly? How will the team measure improvement?

## Areas of support

- Mechanical and electronic subsystem planning
- Sensor and actuator selection
- Structured testing and failure analysis
- Documentation and presentation development
- Team workflow and time management
- Competition-readiness reviews
