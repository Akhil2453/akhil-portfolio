## Purpose

Design Spark helps learners enter a Design Engineering programme with a more active way of seeing the world. The activities are not designed as examination-oriented assignments. They are prompts for observation, curiosity and reflective practice.

## Activity areas

The journey includes a design challenge, design disasters, design failures, sketching, product ideas, product dissection, technology trends, reading and selected videos or films.

## Documentation

Each learner maintains a process document. The emphasis is on what was observed, how ideas changed, what evidence was gathered and what was learned through the activity.
