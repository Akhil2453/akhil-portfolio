## Programme environment

The makerspace operated as more than a laboratory. It was a learning ecosystem where students, teachers and mentors could explore robotics, drones, electronics, 3D printing, coding, AI and design thinking through practical work.

## Responsibilities

The role covered day-to-day operations, people coordination, resource planning, technical mentoring, equipment readiness, safety, curriculum implementation, reporting and stakeholder communication.

## Programme formats

The learning portfolio included short experiential workshops, deeper technical modules, teacher-development sessions and long-duration competition mentoring. Each format was designed around a clear learning progression rather than a collection of disconnected activities.
