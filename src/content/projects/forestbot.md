## System concept

ForestBot combines remote mobility with environmental observation. The architecture includes motor control, ultrasonic sensing, flame and gas detection, alert mechanisms, camera streaming and a web interface for telemetry.

## Learning value

The project demonstrates hardware-software integration, event-driven monitoring, safety considerations, live data handling and human-machine interaction through a browser-based dashboard.
