## Research question

Could live pollution conditions become an input to traffic management rather than remaining only a reporting metric?

The project explored environmental sensing at traffic signals, automated alerts and traffic-control actions. It combined an IoT notification layer with robotic traffic-control prototypes and route-diversion concepts.
