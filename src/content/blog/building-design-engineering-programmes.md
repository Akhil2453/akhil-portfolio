A Design Engineering programme cannot be created by adding a few design-thinking activities to a conventional engineering structure. The programme must align curriculum, pedagogy, assessment, faculty practice, learning spaces and project progression.

This draft will examine the operating systems required to make that alignment visible and sustainable.
