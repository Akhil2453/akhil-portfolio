# Activity-Based Lesson Plan Checklist

Use this checklist before finalising a classroom activity.

## Purpose
- Is the activity directly connected to a learning outcome?
- Is the expected learner action clear?
- Does the activity require thinking, making, analysing or deciding?

## Preparation
- Are all materials and digital tools listed?
- Can the activity be completed within the available time?
- Are safety, accessibility and group-size requirements addressed?

## Facilitation
- Is there a clear opening prompt or problem?
- Are instructions brief enough for learners to begin quickly?
- Are there planned checkpoints for observation and feedback?

## Evidence of learning
- What artefact, decision, explanation or demonstration will show learning?
- Is the rubric aligned with the intended outcome?
- Does the assessment capture both process and result?

## Reflection
- What will learners document after the activity?
- How will the next class use the evidence generated?
